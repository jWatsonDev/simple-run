Ruck & Run

A minimal GPS activity tracker for iPhone. Track a run, walk, or ruck — watch your route draw on the map and review your history.

Getting Started

Choose your activity (Run, Walk, or Ruck) on the main screen, then tap Start. Allow location access when prompted — Ruck & Run uses your GPS to track distance and draw your route. Tap Finish when done. Your activity is saved automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need location access?
Ruck & Run uses GPS to calculate distance, pace, and draw your route on the map. Location data never leaves your device.

Does the app sync with Apple Health?
No. Ruck & Run does not integrate with Apple Health or HealthKit. All data is stored on-device only.

Where is my activity data stored?
All history is stored locally on your device. Ruck & Run does not use any servers or cloud storage.

The map isn't showing my location. What do I do?
Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Ruck & Run and set it to "While Using the App".

Why does the app need background location?
GPS tracking continues when your screen locks during an activity. Without background location, your route and distance would stop recording the moment your screen turns off.
