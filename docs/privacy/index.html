Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Simple Run is designed with privacy in mind. We collect no personal data and operate no servers.

Location Data

Simple Run accesses your device's GPS while you are actively using the app to calculate distance, pace, and draw your route on the map. This location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Run History

Your run history (distance, time, pace, and route coordinates) is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. We have no access to this data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account or login required
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data shared with third parties

Children's Privacy

Simple Run does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
